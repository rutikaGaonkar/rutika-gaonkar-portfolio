About Me

Hello! I'm a Data Science Student

Hi, I'm Rutika Gaonkar, a data science graduate student at the University of Greenwich. I transitioned into data from a marketing background, drawn by its ability to turn complexity into clarity and uncover meaning in what first seems like noise.

What I love most about data is its balance of logic and creativity; there's structure, but also space to explore. Whether it's finding patterns, asking better questions, or building something useful from raw information, I find the process both grounding and exciting.

Now, I'm learning to shape data into stories, tools, and decisions using Python, pandas, SQL and Tableau, and I'm currently exploring machine learning and data storytelling as part of my academic journey.

I'm excited by the potential of data to improve everyday decisions, and I'm here to keep learning, building, and solving meaningful problems.
